# NUSA Chain Whitepaper v1.0

## Abstract

NUSA Chain adalah blockchain Layer-1 yang dirancang untuk mencegah monopoli ekonomi melalui mekanisme Proof-of-Value-Creation (PoVC) dan sistem anti-whale terintegrasi.

## 1.  Masalah yang Diselesaikan

### 1.1 Monopoli di Blockchain Tradisional
- **Proof-of-Work**: Didominasi mining pools besar
- **Proof-of-Stake**: "Rich get richer"
- **DeFi**: Whale manipulation, unfair distribution

### 1.2 Ketimpangan Ekonomi Digital
- 1% holder menguasai 90%+ supply
- Reward sistem menguntungkan modal besar
- Barrier to entry tinggi

## 2. Solusi: NUSA Chain

### 2.1 Proof-of-Value-Creation (PoVC)

PVC Score = Economic + Knowledge + Community + Innovation

### 2.2 Anti-Whale Mechanisms

- Soft Cap: 100,000 NUSA per wallet
- Progressive Transaction Tax
- Dynamic Reward Decay

### 2.3 Three-Layer Architecture

- **L1 (Go)**: Core blockchain
- **L2 (Rust)**: Smart contract VM
- **L3 (Python)**: AI scoring

## 3. Tokenomics

- **Total**: 25,000,000 NUSA (fixed)
- **Distribution**:
  - 50% Public (PoVC Rewards)
  - 25% Community Development
  - 15% Network Reserve
  - 10% Core Team (4-year vesting)

## 4. Governance

On-chain DAO with 1 NUSA = 1 vote (capped at 10K votes per wallet)

## 5.  Roadmap

- Q1 2025: Testnet
- Q2 2025: Mainnet Beta
- Q3 2025: Full Launch
- Q4 2025: Cross-chain Bridges
