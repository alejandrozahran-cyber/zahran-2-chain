# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in NUSA Chain a harassment-free experience for everyone. 

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior ✅
- Being respectful
- Welcoming newcomers
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on community benefit
- Showing empathy

### Unacceptable Behavior ❌
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to: conduct@nusa.chain (coming soon)

All reports will be reviewed and investigated.

## Consequences

1. **Warning**: Private written warning
2. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary ban from interaction
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent ban from the project
